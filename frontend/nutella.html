Nutella Bread

Nutella based snack that you'll be thinking about all day

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1
    tablespoon of Nutella
  • 2
    slices of bread of choice (white, pita, wheat, sour dough)

Instructions

  1. Toast bread
  2. Using a butter knife, spread nutella over warm bread till it covers the top completely
  3. Devour your new favorite dessert
