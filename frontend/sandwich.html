Turkey Sandwich

A to-go snack that can give you a nice boost

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 4
    slices of turkey breast
  • 1
    sliced cheese of choice
  • Fresh lettuce or arugula
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • .25
    of an avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Balsamic vinaigrette
  • Bread of choice

Instructions

  1. Take bread of choice and put the desired amount of sliced turkey on one half
  2. Place the sliced cheese on top of the turkey and put it in the over for 5 mins at 375
  3. Pull out of over and add greens and tomato on top of the cheese
  4. Put some balsamic vinaigrette on the other piece of bread
  5. Put sliced avocado on top of the vinaigrette and add salt and pepper
  6. Combine both halves and enjoy!
