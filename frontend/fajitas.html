Fajitas

A quick, easy dinner to leave you satisfied

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1
    red bell pepper
  • 1
    chicken breast
  • Chicken seasoning
  • 1
    avocado
  • 1
    cup rice
  • 2
    Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Chop up bell peppers and add to pan with olive oil
  2. Slice up chicken breast and add seasoning
  3. Add chicken to pan and add desired seasoning (garlic powder, cumin, salt, pepper)
  4. Warm up/cook rice and put some on a warmed tortilla
  5. When chicken and peppers are finished cooking and taste as desired add to tortilla
  6. Slice avocado and put a few slices on the fajita, too
  7. Close tortilla and enjoy your meal!
