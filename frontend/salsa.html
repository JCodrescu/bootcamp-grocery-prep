Salsa

A delicious snack that goes well with tacos/fajits/quesadillas

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2
    cups chopped tomatoes
  • .25
    cup chopped red onion
  • .25
    cup chopped yellow onion
  • 2
    tablespoons canned green chilies
  • 2
    tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2
    cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1
    tablespoon ground cumin
  • .25
    teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients to a food processor/blender in the order listed above
  2. Salt to taste
  3. Add water if too thick
  4. Grab some chips and enjoy!
