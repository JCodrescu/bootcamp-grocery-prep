Pizza

Tasty pizza to give you a break from your work

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1
    pizza crust
  • 4
    tablespoons of marinara sauce
  • 1
    cup mozerella
  • Peperronis
  • .5
    of a bell pepper
  • A few portobello mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Add marinara sauce to crust spreading across the entire thing
  2. Add mozerella cheese to cover the sauce completely
  3. Throw on desired amount of pepperonis, bell peppers, and mushrooms
  4. Cook in the over for 14 mins at 400
  5. Optional: top with parmesean/arugula/crushed red peppers
  6. Enjoy!
