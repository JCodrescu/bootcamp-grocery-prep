Hummus

A tasty snack to get you through your day

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1
    cup chickpeas
  • 1
    tablespoon Tahini
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • 2
    cloves fresh garlic
  • 1
    tablespoon ground cumin and salt
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients to a food processor/blender in the order listed above
  2. Salt to taste
  3. Add water if too thick
  4. Warm up some pita bread or grab some chips and enjoy!
