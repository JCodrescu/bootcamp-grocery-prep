Ice Cream and Fruit

Healthy or not healthy? That is the question

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 2
    scoops of Ice cream (flavor is up to opinions - I like vanilla most)
  • 1
    cup of fruits of choice (usually blackberries, raspberries, strawberries

Instructions

  1. Slice up fruit
  2. Scoop a few scoops of ice cream into a cup and add fruit
  3. Feast on your cooling dessert
