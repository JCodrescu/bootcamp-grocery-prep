Chicken Pesto Pasta

A fresh tasting meal to brighten your day

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 1
    box of pasta of choice
  • 2
    chicken breasts
  • Chicken seasoning
  • Arugula
  • 5
    fresh mini tomatoes
  • 2
    tablespoons basil pesto

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in separate pot
  2. Slice up chicken breast and add seasoning
  3. Cook chicken in pan with olive oil until thoroughly cooked
  4. Drain pasta and add chicken to the pot
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of pesto and desired amount of arugula/tomatoes
  6. Add parmesean if desired and salt to taste
  7. Enjoy your lunch!
